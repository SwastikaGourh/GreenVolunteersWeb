GREEN VOLUNTEERS
Let's go green together with Green Volunteers!

GREEN VOLUNTEERS was founded by the citizens of Raniganj in 2019, a town in Burdwan dist. West Bengal. Seeing the pollution around us the team decided to do something regarding it. So that's how GREEN VOLUNTEERS was started. Unfortunetly from 2022 to early 2023 it was inactive but from June 2023 it's returning! Yes! It will come back again on the first/second week of June.
